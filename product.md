# Local Context: Chennai Street Food Guide

This product teaches Kiro about Chennai’s local street food culture.

## Popular Foods
- Kothu Parotta: Spicy chopped parotta with gravy
- Sundal: Boiled chickpeas with coconut
- Bajji & Bonda: Deep-fried snacks, popular in evenings
- Atho: Burmese-origin spicy noodle snack

## Best Areas
- Marina Beach: Sundal, Bajji
- T Nagar: Chat items, Atho
- Sowcarpet: North Indian and fusion street food

## Cultural Notes
- Evening snacks are most popular between 4 PM – 8 PM
- People prefer spicy food
- Street food is affordable and quick
